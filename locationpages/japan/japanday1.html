Day 1

May 14th, 2025

We arrived in Japan at around 10:30 AM. Once we checked into our hotel, it was already around 12PM. We were very hungry so we explored the surrounding area. Prior to the trip, we made a list of possible restaurants in Osaka. However, all of these restaurants were too far and since we wanted ramen, we searched for some nice ramen spots. Eventually we settled for a a nice and nearby ramen restaurant called Mugi X Tori.The ramen was very delicious. The broth was Rich and creamy, unlike any other ramen I've ever had. The flavour was strong and pronounced, and the meat was very tender and fatty.
